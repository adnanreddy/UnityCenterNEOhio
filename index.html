The Unity Center of Northeast Ohio is a Shia masjid serving greater Cleveland and Akron community. Founded in 2005 from a conglomeration of smaller communities, the masjid found a permanent home in 201X in Northfield, Ohio. Programming is offered in English, Arabic, Farsi and Urdu to serve our diverse community. We look forward to welcoming you to our masjid!
